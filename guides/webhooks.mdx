## Webhooks

Webhooks are a way to notify you when certain events happen in your workspace this is useful for performing actions such as sending out newsletters, revalidating pages, or even sending a Slack notification.

### Creating a Webhook

To create a webhook, you can go to the webhooks page in the sidebar and click on the "Create Webhook" button.
