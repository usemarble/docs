## Setup

To integrate MarbleCMS in your Next.js application, you first need to create a Next.js app. If you don't have one, you can check out the [Next.js documentation](https://nextjs.org/docs/getting-started) to get started. or use our [nextjs blog template](https://github.com/taqh/nextjs-example) to get started the setup intructions are in the Readme.
