## Setup

To integrate MarbleCMS in your Astro application, you first need an astro site. If you don't have one, you can check out the [Astro documentation](https://docs.astro.build/en/tutorial/0-introduction/) to get started. or use our [astro blog template](https://github.com/taqh/astro-example) to get started the setup intructions are in the Readme.
