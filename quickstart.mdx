## Create an account

To get stated writing your blog, you need to create a marblecms account. head over to [the website](https://marblecms.com) and hit the signup button to begin.

## Create a workspace

After you have created an account you will be redirected to a page where you can create a workspace.

![Create a workspace](/images/create-workspace.png)

## What is a workspace?

You can think of a workspace as a website. each new workspace would hold content for a new website.

## Fill in the details

You can name your workspace whatever you want. Once you have named your workspace, hit the create workspace button.

![Fill in the details](/images/fill-in-details.png)

## The dashboard

Once you have created a workspace, you will be redirected to the dashboard.

![The dashboard](/images/dashboard.png)

## Create a new blog

Once you have created a workspace, you can create your first blog.
